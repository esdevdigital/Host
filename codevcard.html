Expertise & Vision

Mr. Ahmad Chakik, Chairman, is the founder of OPAL MINING CO LTD and a prominent Lebanese businessman with over 3 decades of experience in Investment, Mining, and Financial Management across Africa and the UAE. He possesses extensive strategic links across China, the Middle East, Asia, and Africa.

His multi-disciplinary expertise spans diverse fields including trade, oil & gas, banking, mining, food, technology, and agriculture. He is also the founder or shareholder of several multinational companies, including Active Management Consultant FZC, Everon Building Cleaning Services LLC, and Bianco Plus Quarries Co. Ltd. His strategic insight drives Opal Management Consultancy's vision for a smarter, safer, and more sustainable future.
